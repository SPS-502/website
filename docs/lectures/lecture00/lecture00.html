AI and this course

  • You have an opportunity to grow your skills well beyond what we do in class this semester.
  • Over-reliance on AI will rob you of that opportunity. You’re investing a tremendous amount of your time to this course and your education, generally.
  • If you can’t do anything more than what AI/LLM can do then it CAN replace you
